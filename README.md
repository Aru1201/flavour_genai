# flavour_genai
Flavour Fusion is a recipe blog powered by AI that combines culinary creativity with data-driven insights. It uses AI to generate unique recipes, suggest flavor pairings, and personalize content for readers. The blog can appeal to home cooks, foodies, and culinary experimenters looking for new inspirations.

Key Features
a. AI-Generated Recipes

Users can input ingredients, dietary preferences, or cuisine type.

AI produces complete recipes with step-by-step instructions, cooking times, and difficulty levels.

Example: “Create a vegan Italian dinner with mushrooms and tofu.”

b. Flavor Pairing Insights

AI recommends unexpected ingredient combinations based on chemical flavor profiles and global cuisine trends.

Can include a “Surprise Me” feature for adventurous cooks.

c. Personalized Recommendations

Tracks user preferences: dietary restrictions, favorite cuisines, cooking skill level.

Suggests recipes tailored to individual taste profiles.

d. Interactive Blog Content

AI can write engaging articles about trends, nutrition tips, or seasonal ingredients.

Could include storytelling: e.g., “The history of Saffron in Mediterranean cooking” with AI-generated narratives.

e. Visual & Multimedia Enhancements

AI-assisted food photography suggestions or automated image enhancements.

Step-by-step video generation (optional), with AI voiceovers for instructions.

3. Monetization Opportunities

Sponsored content with food brands.

Affiliate links for kitchen tools or specialty ingredients.

Premium subscriptions for AI recipe generation and personalized meal planning.

4. Technical Stack Suggestions

Backend AI: GPT-based model for recipe generation.

Frontend: WordPress or Webflow with interactive widgets.

Database: Store user profiles, preferences, and recipe history.

Analytics: Track engagement and popular recipe trends.

5. Unique Selling Points

Combines human creativity with AI efficiency.

Offers dynamic, adaptive, and personalized cooking experiences.

Helps users experiment safely with unusual flavors or dietary constraints.
